About Rigo-AI

We're revolutionizing agriculture by making advanced soil analysis accessible to every farmer through artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

At Rigo-AI, we believe that technology should empower farmers, not complicate their lives. Traditional soil testing is expensive, time-consuming, and often inaccessible to small-scale farmers.

We're changing that by leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence to provide instant, accurate soil analysis from a simple photograph. Our mission is to democratize agricultural technology and help farmers make data-driven decisions that improve yields and sustainability.

The Technology

Rigo-AI uses advanced computer vision and machine learning models trained on thousands of soil samples from across Africa. Our AI can identify:

  • Soil Type: Clay, sandy, loamy, silty, peaty, or chalky
  • Nutrient Levels: Nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium estimates
  • pH Balance: Acidity or alkalinity indicators
  • Recommendations: Crop suggestions and soil improvement tips

How to Get the Best Results

Follow these tips for accurate soil analysis

Good Lighting

Take photos in natural daylight for best color accuracy

Clean Sample

Remove debris, rocks, and plant matter from the soil

Close-Up Shot

Fill the frame with soil, avoid shadows and reflections

Flat Surface

Spread soil evenly on a flat, neutral-colored surface
